School Church
Church (building), a building for Christian religious activities. Church (congregation), a local congregation of a Christian denomination. Church service, a formalized period of Christian communal worship. Christian denomination, a Christian organization with distinct doctrine and practice.



School Library

A school library is a building within a school that houses a collection of books, audio-visual materials, and other content. It provides a variety of resources to students, staff, and often, parents of a public or private school.



School Auditorium

An auditorium is also known as an assembly hall. It is an essential part of a school as it has multiple uses to organize celebrations and recreational activities. A majority of schools use their auditorium on a daily basis for their morning prayers and assembly.
